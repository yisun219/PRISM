I am an undergraduate researcher at the School of Microelectronics, South China University of Technology. During Spring 2026, I studied computer architecture and digital integrated-circuit design as a visiting student at the University of California, Berkeley.

My research centers on **AI for architecture** and **architecture for AI**. I am particularly interested in LLM-guided CPU microarchitecture, automated accelerator generation, and hardware-software co-design for emerging AI workloads. My earlier work in RF and mixed-signal circuit design continues to shape how I approach architecture: from algorithms and dataflows down to physical implementation constraints.

I currently work with [Prof. Jieru Zhao](https://zjru.github.io/) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University on automated spatial-accelerator generation. I have also worked with [Prof. Xiang Yi](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?hl=en&user=QgYwNVgAAAAJ), [Prof. Li Gao](https://www.linkedin.com/in/li-gao-582340b3/), and [Prof. Enyi Yao](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?hl=en&user=JNbWKlIAAAAJ) on neural-network-based digital predistortion and integrated-circuit design.
